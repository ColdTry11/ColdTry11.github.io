About Me

Hello, my name is Kris Kyle Kekoa Montemayor, and I am an aspiring game developer. Video games have always been a passion of mine, and as I grew up, I became increasingly fascinated with the inner workings of computers. I pursued this interest by taking computer classes in high school, where I learned how to code. It was then that I realized I could use this newfound skill to bring my own game ideas to life. Then I continued by majoring in Computer Science when I got into College.

Since then, I have been dedicated to improving my skills as a developer, with the ultimate goal of creating engaging and immersive video game experiences. It's not always easy, but I am committed to working hard and continually learning to achieve my dreams. I can't wait to see what the future holds for me in the world of game development.
